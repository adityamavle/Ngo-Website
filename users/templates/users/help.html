Sign Up Module


VOs/NGOs need to sign up with the Portal only once in order to avail the facilities/features made available through the Portal. They can update their profile any time they wish to. They need to create their unique login user ID. and profile. Copy of the registration certificate should be scanned in the PDF format or JPG format (should be less than 2 MB size) and submitted under the concerned option. Multiple selections are possible using the 'Control' key. On selection of a particular state, you would get the list of districts of the selected state, for selection of districts working in.


Sections of this Module


NGO Directory This section of the module provides a sector -wise list of NGOs in Pune. It allows you to search for NGOs by sector and by name.
